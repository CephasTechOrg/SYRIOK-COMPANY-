Our Story

With a legacy dating back to 2010, SIRYORK COMPANY LIMITED has established itself as a premier authority in modern roofing and construction solutions across Ghana.

Founded in Kumasi Edweso Ghana, we specialize in delivering premium roofing systems that perfectly blend durability with aesthetic excellence. Our commitment to quality has made us the preferred choice for residential, commercial, and governmental projects.

Beyond installation, we empower the next generation of roofing professionals through comprehensive training programs and provide expert consultancy services for construction projects of all scales.

Our portfolio includes landmark collaborations with the Ghanaian government, including stadiums, churches, and large-scale facilities accommodating over 20,000 people. We provide specialized guidance on air usage, ventilation systems, and roofing orientations to ensure optimal performance and comfort.
